eCommerce

I worked with two other people to build this application, which is a simple eCommerce store. Users can choose to be both buyer and seller, or just a buyer. In addition to the basic model, view, and controller setup, I created the price watch feature. Once a user sets a "price watch" on an item, they receive an email when the item's price drops below the one they specified in their "price watch". I created this feature using Sidekiq, Sidetiq, Redis, and ActionMailer.
